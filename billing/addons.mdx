Add-ons let you expand a workspace's capabilities beyond what its base plan provides. Whether a client needs an extra bot, more contact slots, or a custom domain, you can attach individual add-ons to any workspace — each one deducted from your wallet balance the moment it is applied.

## Available Add-ons

The table below lists every add-on available in Botky, its internal identifier, default price, and what it does.

| Add-on ID | Name | Default Price | Description |
|-----------|------|--------------|-------------|
| `bot` | Extra Bot | $10 | Adds one additional bot to the workspace |
| `member` | Extra Member | $10 | Adds one additional team member seat |
| `bot_user` | 1,000 Bot Users | $10 | Adds 1,000 contact/user slots |
| `bot_user_large` | 10,000 Bot Users | $60 | Adds 10,000 contact/user slots |
| `lists` | Tickets / Lists | $30 | Enables the tickets and lists feature |
| `inbound_webhook` | Inbound Webhook | $40 | Enables inbound webhook integrations |
| `timeout` | Timeout | $20 | Enables the conversation timeout feature |
| `custom_domain` | Custom Domain | $15 | Allows a custom domain for the workspace |

<Note>
Default prices are configurable by your administrator via the Custom Pricing settings. The actual prices shown in your dashboard may differ from the defaults listed above. See [Custom Pricing](/configuration/custom-pricing) for details.
</Note>

## How Add-on Costs Work

**Wallet deduction on activation** — When you apply an add-on to a workspace, Botky immediately deducts the add-on's price from your wallet balance and activates the feature on the workspace at the same time.

**Insufficient balance blocks the operation** — If your wallet does not have enough funds to cover the add-on cost, Botky blocks the operation and displays an **"Insufficient Balance"** error showing how much is required versus how much you have available. Top up your wallet and try again.

**Automatic refund on activation failure** — If Botky deducts the cost from your wallet but the UChat API call fails (for example, due to a temporary service error), the full amount is automatically refunded to your wallet. You are never charged for an add-on that was not successfully activated.

**Removing an add-on does not trigger a refund** — Removing an add-on deactivates it on the workspace, but the cost already deducted from your wallet when it was applied is not returned. Plan your add-on purchases accordingly.

<Warning>
Add-on purchases are non-refundable within Botky except in the case of an activation failure (which is refunded automatically). If you accidentally apply an add-on, contact your administrator — they can adjust your wallet balance manually, but a successful add-on removal will not credit your balance automatically.
</Warning>

## Managing Add-ons on a Workspace

To add or remove add-ons on a specific client workspace, open the workspace detail view from your dashboard. See [Workspaces](/dashboard/workspaces) for step-by-step instructions.

To configure custom prices for add-ons across your account, see [Custom Pricing](/configuration/custom-pricing).
