Plans determine what each client workspace can do. When you create or update a workspace in Botky, you select a plan that sets the baseline capabilities — number of bots, contact slots, team members, and features — and Botky deducts the corresponding cost from your wallet at that moment.

## Available Plans

The table below lists every plan preset available in Botky along with its default price and a summary of what it includes.

| Plan | Default Price | Description |
|------|--------------|-------------|
| Business | $20 | Base plan — 1 bot, 1,000 users, 5 members |
| Business Lite | $45 | Mid-tier combo plan with expanded limits |
| Business Plus | $99 | Advanced combo plan for growing clients |
| Business Large | $149 | Large-scale combo plan for high-volume needs |
| Business Yearly | $990 | Annual plan offering significant savings over monthly billing |
| Free | $0 | Free tier — no wallet deduction |

<Note>
The prices shown above are global defaults. Your administrator may configure custom per-user pricing, so the amounts displayed in your dashboard may differ from the defaults listed here.
</Note>

## Custom Plans

If none of the preset plans match a client's exact needs, Botky can calculate a custom price on the fly. Custom pricing starts with the Business base and adds costs for each extra resource you configure on top of it.

### Custom Plan Pricing Formula

<Steps>
  <Step title="Start with the Business base price">
    Every custom plan begins at the Business plan price ($20 by default).
  </Step>
  <Step title="Add costs for extra bots">
    Multiply the number of additional bots by the per-bot price (default $10 each).
  </Step>
  <Step title="Add costs for extra members">
    Multiply the number of additional team member seats by the per-member price (default $10 each).
  </Step>
  <Step title="Add costs for extra user blocks">
    Users are billed in blocks of 1,000. Multiply the number of additional 1k-user blocks by the per-block price (default $10 per 1,000 users).
  </Step>
  <Step title="Sum the totals">
    The custom plan price is the sum of all the above. Botky calculates and displays this total before you confirm the workspace creation or plan change.
  </Step>
</Steps>

**Example:** A client needs 3 bots, 8 members, and 5,000 users.

| Component | Calculation | Cost |
|-----------|------------|------|
| Business base | — | $20 |
| Extra bots (2 above base) | 2 × $10 | $20 |
| Extra members (3 above base of 5) | 3 × $10 | $30 |
| Extra user blocks (4 above base 1k) | 4 × $10 | $40 |
| **Total** | | **$110** |

<Tip>
Use a plan preset whenever possible — they are optimised bundles that typically cost less than building an equivalent custom configuration from scratch.
</Tip>

## Changing a Plan

You can upgrade or change a client's plan at any time from the workspace detail view. The new plan's price is deducted from your wallet when you confirm the change. See [Workspaces](/dashboard/workspaces) for step-by-step instructions.
