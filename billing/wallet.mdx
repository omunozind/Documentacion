The Botky wallet is a pre-funded USD balance that powers every billing operation on your dashboard. Instead of entering payment details each time you create a workspace or change a plan, you maintain a running balance that Botky deducts from automatically — keeping operations fast and your clients uninterrupted.

## How the Wallet Works

Your current balance appears in the dashboard header next to the green wallet icon, so you always know where you stand before taking action.

Every billable operation deducts the corresponding cost from your balance the moment you confirm it:

- **Creating a workspace** — deducts the price of the selected plan
- **Changing a plan** — deducts the new plan's price
- **Adding an add-on** — deducts the add-on's price

If your balance is insufficient at the time of an operation, Botky blocks the action and displays an **"Insufficient Balance"** error showing exactly how much you need. No partial charges are applied.

## Balance Indicator Colors

The wallet icon in the header changes color to signal your balance state at a glance:

| Color | Meaning |
|-------|---------|
| 🟢 Green | You have a positive balance — operations can proceed |
| ⚪ Gray | Your balance is exactly zero — top up before your next operation |
| 🔴 Red | Your balance is negative — contact your administrator immediately |

<Warning>
A red (negative) balance means a billing inconsistency has occurred. You cannot perform new billable operations until your administrator resolves and restores your balance.
</Warning>

## Wallet Operations & Costs

The table below shows the default cost deducted for each operation. Your administrator may configure custom pricing, so the amounts you see in the dashboard may differ.

| Operation | Default Cost |
|-----------|-------------|
| Create Workspace — Business | $20 |
| Create Workspace — Business Lite | $45 |
| Create Workspace — Business Plus | $99 |
| Create Workspace — Business Large | $149 |
| Create Workspace — Business Yearly | $990 |
| Change Plan | Price of the new plan |
| Add-on purchase | Varies — see the [Add-ons](/billing/addons) page |

<Note>
Your administrator can adjust your wallet balance manually at any time and can also set custom prices for plans and add-ons. The prices shown above are global defaults.
</Note>

## Keeping Your Wallet Funded

<Warning>
If you plan to perform bulk operations — such as creating multiple workspaces in a single session — ensure your wallet has enough balance to cover all operations before you begin. A mid-batch failure due to insufficient funds will leave some workspaces created and others not.
</Warning>

<Tip>
Add a comfortable buffer above your expected spend. For example, if you plan to create five Business Plus workspaces ($99 each), top up at least $495 plus a small reserve before starting.
</Tip>

To add funds to your wallet, see the [Top-ups](/billing/topups) guide.
