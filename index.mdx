Botky is a WordPress plugin built specifically for UChat reseller partners. It gives you a fully branded dashboard to create and manage client workspaces, control plans and add-ons, and handle billing — all from inside your own WordPress site. Connect your UChat Partner API token once and you're ready to onboard clients the same day.

## Get Up and Running

Follow these four steps to go from a fresh WordPress install to managing your first client workspace.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Install the Plugin">
    Upload the Botky ZIP file through **Plugins → Add New → Upload Plugin** in your WordPress admin and click **Activate Plugin**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Connect Your API Token">
    Open the **Settings** tab inside the Partner Connect dashboard, paste your UChat Partner API token, and click **Save**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Top Up Your Wallet">
    Go to the **Top-ups** tab, enter the amount you want to add, and complete the Stripe checkout. Your wallet balance updates immediately.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Create Your First Workspace">
    Switch to the **Workspaces** tab, click **New**, fill in the client's details, choose a plan, and click **Create**.
  </Step>
</Steps>
