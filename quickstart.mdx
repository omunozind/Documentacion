This guide walks you through everything you need to go from zero to your first active client workspace. By the end you will have the plugin installed, your UChat Partner API token connected, your wallet funded, and a workspace provisioned — all in a single sitting.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Install the Plugin">
    Upload and activate Botky from your WordPress admin panel.

    1. In your WordPress admin, go to **Plugins → Add New**.
    2. Click **Upload Plugin** at the top of the page.
    3. Choose the `botky.zip` file you received from your provider and click **Install Now**.
    4. Once installation completes, click **Activate Plugin**.

    A new **Partner Connect** item appears in your WordPress admin sidebar. Click it to open the dashboard.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Connect Your API Token">
    Link Botky to your UChat Partner account so it can call the API on your behalf.

    1. Inside the Partner Connect dashboard, click the **Settings** tab.
    2. Paste your **UChat Partner Token** into the API token field.
       - Find your token in your UChat partner account at [uchat.com.au](https://uchat.com.au) under your partner profile settings.
    3. Click **Save**.

    Botky stores the token securely in your WordPress database. A confirmation message confirms the token was saved successfully.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Top Up Your Wallet">
    Add funds to your Botky wallet so you have balance available to cover workspace costs.

    1. Click the **Top-ups** tab in the Partner Connect dashboard.
    2. Enter the dollar amount you want to add to your wallet.
    3. Click **Pay** — you are redirected to a Stripe-hosted checkout page.
    4. Complete the payment using a credit or debit card.

    After a successful payment, Stripe sends a confirmation back to Botky and your wallet balance updates immediately. You can return to the **Wallet** tab at any time to review your current balance and transaction history.

    <Note>
      Your wallet balance must be sufficient to cover the cost of the plan you assign before you can create or change a workspace. Check plan costs in the **Plans** tab before proceeding.
    </Note>
  </Step>

  <Step title="Create a Workspace">
    Provision a new UChat workspace for a client using their admin user details.

    1. Click the **Workspaces** tab.
    2. Click the **New** button.
    3. Fill in the workspace form:

    | Field | Description |
    |---|---|
    | **First Name** | Client admin's first name |
    | **Last Name** | Client admin's last name |
    | **Email** | Client admin's email address (used to log in to UChat) |
    | **Password** | Initial password for the client's UChat account |
    | **Phone** | Client admin's phone number |
    | **Company** | Client's company or brand name |
    | **Language** | Interface language for the workspace (`en` or `es`) |
    | **Trial Days** | Number of free trial days before billing starts |

    4. Select a **Plan** from the dropdown.
    5. Click **Create**.

    Botky calls the UChat Partner API, provisions the workspace, and deducts the plan cost from your wallet. The new workspace appears in the workspaces table within a few seconds.
  </Step>
</Steps>
